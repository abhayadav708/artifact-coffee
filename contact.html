Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take reservations?

We operate on a first-come, first-served basis during regular hours (8AM-3PM/4PM). For private evening events, reservations are required.

Can I order ahead for pickup?

Yes! You can place pickup orders through our Toast online ordering page. This is the best way to skip the line during busy times.

Do you have WiFi?

Yes, we offer free WiFi to our customers. Please ask a staff member for the password.

Are you able to accommodate dietary restrictions?

We do our best to accommodate various dietary needs. Our seasonal menu includes vegetarian and vegan options. Please speak with our staff about specific allergies or restrictions.

Do you sell coffee beans to take home?

Yes! We sell bags of our freshly roasted coffee beans. Ask your barista about current offerings.

Is there parking available?

Yes, there is street parking available around our location on Union Ave.
