Our Story

Coffee, community, and craft

More Than Coffee

Artifact Coffee is a celebration of what happens when quality ingredients, skilled hands, and genuine care come together. We're part of Baltimore's fabric, committed to serving our neighbors while supporting the farmers and producers who make our work possible.

The Coffee

Every cup starts with carefully sourced beans, roasted to highlight their unique characteristics. We work directly with importers who share our values of fair trade and sustainability, ensuring that the farmers who grow our coffee are paid fairly and treated with respect.

Our roasting philosophy is simple: let the beans speak for themselves. We roast to bring out the natural sweetness, acidity, and complexity that make each origin special.

The Food

Our kitchen operates on the same principles as our coffee program: seasonal, local, and made from scratch. We build relationships with nearby farms and producers, creating menus that change with what's freshest and best.

This approach means our menu is always evolving, but it also means you're getting ingredients at their peak, prepared by people who care about the craft of cooking.

The Community

We're proud to call Baltimore home. This city's creative energy, resilience, and diversity inspire us daily. Being a good neighbor means more than serving great coffee—it means supporting local causes, providing good jobs, and creating a space where everyone feels welcome.

Whether you're stopping in for your morning coffee, working on your laptop, or celebrating a special occasion at one of our evening events, we want Artifact to feel like your place too.

The Environment

Caring for our environment isn't optional—it's essential. We use compostable packaging for our catering and takeaway orders, minimize food waste through careful planning, and prioritize working with producers who practice sustainable farming.

Every choice we make, from our coffee sourcing to our to-go containers, is made with consideration for our environmental impact.

The Woodberry Kitchen Family

Artifact Coffee shares its DNA with Woodberry Kitchen, Baltimore's pioneering farm-to-table restaurant. The same commitment to local sourcing, seasonal cooking, and community support that defines Woodberry Kitchen runs through everything we do at Artifact.

This connection allows us to work with an incredible network of local farmers, bakers, and producers, giving us access to the best ingredients Maryland has to offer.
